ABOUT

My name is Oliver Hu and I am a senior at Brown University studying computer science and music. Last summer, I interned at EF Education First as a full-stack developer, where I worked on web development using React/Redux and the .NET framework. Before that, I interned at Down Dog App, where I worked on UI/UX design, iOS and Android development.

Aside from software development, I have a special interest in design and entrepreneurship. To me, design is all about problem-solving. Good design manifests itself in solutions where every detail has purpose and intent, and where function is valued over form with minimal sacrifice of elegance. It makes sense, then, that design and entrepreneurship go hand-in-hand; the entrepreneur identifies the problem and creates an idea for a solution, and the designer realizes the solution. In our increasingly technologized world, the software developer is becoming an evermore vital third piece, being the one who implements the solution.

What I've described above is the way I approach software development, and the space that I desire to be a part of. I believe that the combination of entrepreneurship, design, and software engineering yields creative solutions to many real-world problems. As such, I tend to gravitate towards front-end development.
